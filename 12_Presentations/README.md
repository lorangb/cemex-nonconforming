# 12 — Presentations & Public Brief Media

Public-facing presentation materials making the case to reverse the rescission and terminate
CEMEX Lyons' nonconforming use. These are advocacy materials, not primary evidence — every
factual claim traces back to the documents in the numbered evidence folders (see `INDEX.csv`
and `TIMELINE.md`).

## Contents

- **`CEMEX_Lyons_Reverse_Rescission_Traffic_Brief.pptx` / `.pdf`** — the current public legal
  brief, centered on reversing the May 2026 rescission and the 1994-vs-today traffic argument
  (conveyor/local supply in 1994 vs. trucking today; the Landis report's omission of 1994).
- **`CEMEX_Lyons_Nonconforming_Use_Legal_Brief_Public.pptx` / `.pdf`** — the broader
  nonconforming-use brief (expansion, intensification, and discontinuance arguments).
- **`CEMEX_Rescission_Appeal.pdf`** — the rescission-appeal one-pager / handout.
- **`CEMEX_Rescission_Appeal.mp4`** — a short video presentation of the appeal.
- **`The_Fight_to_Close_CEMEX_Lyons.m4a`** — an audio overview of the campaign and the case.
- **`Analysis_of_Plant_Traffic_Appeal.png`** — a graphic summarizing the traffic analysis.

> An interactive HTML version of the brief lives in `00_Public_Legal_Brief/`, and copies of
> these presentation files are mirrored there so the brief folder is a one-stop public package.
