# Contributing
Pick a folder, read the primary documents, and open an issue or PR with what you find —
a corrected date, a clearer filename, a missing public record, or analysis of how a
structure, permit revision, or determination bears on the nonconforming-use question
(folders 02, 03, 06, 07, 08 are most central). Use `INDEX.csv` and `TIMELINE.md` to navigate.
Ground rules: primary sources only; cite provenance; add nothing private or personally
identifying.
