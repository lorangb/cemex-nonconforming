# 11 — Reference & Research

Background and supporting material that bears on CEMEX's operation in the area or the
non-compliance claims.

- **Cement_Kiln_Dust/** — references on cement kiln dust (CKD).
- **Emissions_and_Health/** — emissions and public-health materials (e.g., mercury, kiln
  environmental-justice analyses).
- **Comparison_Plants/** — other cement operations used for comparison, including plants that
  import/truck limestone (relevant to the "must mine locally" question).
- **CEMEX_Corporate/** — CEMEX corporate and greenhouse-gas materials.
- **Historical/** — the Colorado Geological Survey publication, mineral-activity reports, and a
  1998 Southdown newsletter.
- **SOSVV/** — Save Our St. Vrain Valley materials on the environmental and health impacts of
  cement manufacturing.

> General cement-industry **decarbonization** research was set aside under `_Not_Relevant/`.
