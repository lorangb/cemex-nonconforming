# 04 — Dowe Flats Mining & Reclamation (Permit M-1993-041)

The Dowe Flats expansion area east of Lyons, where quarrying continued after the original
Lyons pit.

Includes the 1993 special-use mining & reclamation application and its referral/review record,
the 1994 approval (AM01), the 2020 covenant termination, the CEMEX purchase-option agreement,
reclamation cost records and revisions, and the June 2022 mining-permit discussion materials.
